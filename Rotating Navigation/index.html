Bhanu Pratap

Ghar har insaan ki basic zarurat hoti hai. Ghar sirf ek building ya structure nahi hota, balki ek jagah hoti hai jahan hum apne parivaar ke saath pyaar, khushi aur suraksha mehsoos karte hain. Ghar humein dhoop, barish, thand aur dhoop se bachata hai aur ek sukoon bhari jagah deta hai jahan hum apna din khatam karke chain ki neend lete hain.Har kisi ka sapna hota hai apna ek khud ka ghar ho. Koi flat mein rehta hai, koi bungalow mein, aur koi chhoti si jhopdi mein, lekin har ghar ka apna ek importance hota hai. Shehron mein log zyada tar apartments mein rehte hain, jabki gaon mein log independent houses mein rehte hain.

My Future House Will be Like this

Ghar ko sundar banana har kisi ki koshish hoti hai. Log apne ghar ko paint karte hain, furniture lagate hain, garden banate hain aur use sajate hain. Par sabse zaroori cheez yeh hai ki ghar ke andar pyar aur samajh bani rahe, tabhi woh ek "ghar" kehlaata hai.Ghar ek aisi jagah hoti hai jahan hum apne jeevan ke sabse ache pal bitate hain. Isiliye kehna galat nahi hoga ki "ghar jaisa kuch nahi"
